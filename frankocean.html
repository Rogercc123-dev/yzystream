    The Lonny Breaux Collection is an unofficial, fan-made compilation of demos that Frank Ocean put out when he was mostly a songwriter for other artists, which was before he went by his Frank Ocean moniker and instead by the titular "Lonny Breaux." The songs were leaked over the years and compiled by fans on KanyeToThe forums. This project is occasionally misunderstood as being an installment in Frank Ocean's official discography, but it is not.
