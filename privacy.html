Log Files

YZY STREAM follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

Cookies

Like any other website, YZY STREAM uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense on our website. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our site or other websites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.
